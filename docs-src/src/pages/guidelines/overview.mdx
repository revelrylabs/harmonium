<p class="rev-Lead">Harmonium is a Design System for teams that move fast. Scalable design tokens, accessible markup, and a comprehensive component library allow for harmonious user experiences.</p>
<br/>

      </div>
      <div class="rev-Col rev-Col--small12 rev-Col--large5 SectionDivider"> 
        <h6>Methods</h6>
        <ul class="BulletedList">

          <li>Help others see the big picture and plan for scale</li>
          <li>Create design solutions across different media</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="rev-Card rev-Card--large">

        <h3>Accessible</h3>
        <p><strong>We’re stronger together.</strong> Our designs should be accessible and inclusive to all, regardless of ability, culture, location, or quality of internet access.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="rev-Col rev-Col--large5 SectionDivider"> 
        <h6>Methods</h6>
        <ul class="BulletedList">
          <li>Maintain accessibility standards</li>
          <li>Build responsively</li>
          <li>Understand your audience and build for their needs</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>


  <div class="rev-Card rev-Card--large">

        <h3>Consistent</h3>
        <p><strong>Consistency breeds stability.</strong> Maintain consistent standards and develop reusable patterns that allow for a predictable user environment.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="rev-Col rev-Col--large5 SectionDivider"> 
        <h6>Methods</h6>
        <ul class="BulletedList">
          <li>Architect reusable design patterns, components, and templates</li>
          <li>Minimize users’ cognitive loads and decision-making time</li>
          <li>Enable users to feel confident when interacting with our products</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

      <div class="rev-Col rev-Col--large5 SectionDivider">
